Fun Facts about bears



★Brown Bear★

  • They can run up to 40 to 50 km/h
  • Brown bears have an incredible sense of smell, better than most dogs
  • They can weigh over 600 kg depending on the region
  • Famous sub species being grizzly and kodiak
  • Brown bears can interbreed with polar bears
  • They communiacate through pheromones excreted from the feet

★Black Bear★

  • They arent always black they can be brown, cinnamon, or even white
  • They are skiled climbers
  • They are less aggressive than most other bear species
  • They have the most variation in their diets eating fish berries insects or honey
  • They have the longest lasting memory for food sources amongst all the bears
  • White brown bears are typically caled spirit bears

★Polar Bear★

  • They are the largest land carnivores on Earth
  • Their fur isnt actually white its transparent, reflecting light from the snow and sun
  • Underneath their fur their skin is black to absorb heat
  • their strong swimmers and can swm for hundreds of kilometers if the needs be
  • They can smell prey up to 1km away

★Sun Bears★

  • The smallest of all the bear species
  • Their tongues can be upto 25cm long
  • Their names come from the golden sun shape on their chest fur
  • Sun bears are called sloth bears at times due to how much time they spend in trees
  • Similar to badgers their loose skin prevents critical injuries from attacking predators
  • They tend to be nocturnal but that can change based on the location of their habitat
